Important Medical Disclaimer

Heart Portal is an educational resource and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider, cardiologist, or registered dietitian before making significant changes to your diet or treatment plan. Every person's heart failure journey is unique, and your medical team is best positioned to provide personalized guidance.
